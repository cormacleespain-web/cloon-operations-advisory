# Brief — cloon-landing

**Style signals:** "simple", "credible", grey/green (farming in the Burren, Co. Clare
limestone landscape), geometric facets hinting at engineering/operations. Professional
solo-consultant marketing site — not selling through it.

**References:** AI-generated temp logo (cream bg, faceted navy "C", slate blue-grey +
sage green facets) — used as **colour/mood inspiration only**, not to be copied.

**Audience:** prospective clients of a supply-chain / operations advisory practice
(Conor Lee). Also an immediate audience: a meeting where this site is shown as an
"example outcome" first shot.

**Chosen taste authority:** `brand` (identity/palette/voice) + `ui-ux-pro-max`
(systematic design system) — existing-brand-assets branch of the clash-resolution
decision framework (logo + palette + rationale already exist).

**Suppressed for this task:** `minimalist-ui`, `high-end-visual-design`,
`industrial-brutalist-ui`, `gpt-taste`, `stitch-design-taste`, `design-taste-frontend-v1`
— one taste authority per surface (clash-resolution suppression table).

**Motion:** `emil-design-eng` (restrained; not a GSAP stack). Reviewed by
`review-animations` at Stage 7.
